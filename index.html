How old is your towel？
你的毛巾几岁了？

introduction
作品介绍

Thrift has always been regarded as a traditional virtue of Chinese people, but do you know, sometimes thrift can't bring benefits.. But the use value of goods in life tends to decrease with the change of time, which is often difficult for people to detect. Sometimes they think they save money, but they do harm to their health. This illusion of saving is not desirable. So I hope to design a product that can quickly show the loss of the use value of goods, so as to urge people to think more about whether they are excessively frugal. To help people learn to live a better life.

勤俭节约一直被视为中国人的一种传统美德但你知道吗，有些时候勤俭节约并不能带来好处。生活中物品的使用价值往往会随着时间的变化而减少，但是人们往往难以察觉。有时候自认为节省了财富，却造成了对身体的损害。这种节省的幻觉是不可取的。所以我希望设计一款能够快速表现物品使用价值流失的产品，去促使人们多思考，是否过度节俭。从而帮助人们学会更好的生活。

Design sketch设计草图

The change of towel is the "growth" of pattern on towel. But I don't want to make the patterns too beautiful. I want to make these "growing" patterns uncomfortable. The first pattern is to imitate the growth of mold, because they are easy to associate with corruption, not fresh. The second is to imitate the rust of iron. They are very reminiscent of the passage of time, greasy dirt and corrosion. The third is a bright warning color. Many animals will grow bright patterns to intimidate their natural enemies. I want to use these uncomfortable patterns to suggest that users should continue to use towels. It can also give life to the towel and let users feel its life span.

毛巾的变化就是毛巾上花纹的“生长”。但我并不想让花纹变得过分美观，我想让这些“生长”的花纹让人有些不适感。第一种花纹是在模仿霉菌的生长，因为它们很容易让人联想到腐败，不新鲜。第二种是在模仿铁的生锈。它们很容易让人联想到时间的流逝、油腻肮脏以及腐蚀。第三种是鲜亮的警示色。很多动物会生长出鲜艳的花纹去恐吓天敌。我想通过这些让人“不适的花纹”的生长去暗示使用者们思考毛巾是否应该继续使用。也可以赋予这条毛巾生命力，让使用者们感受到它们的寿命。

Experiment
实验

I began to restore the growth of simulated patterns from the biological, chemical and physical aspects. Using the different characteristics of different materials to do pattern growth simulation experiment in reality.
